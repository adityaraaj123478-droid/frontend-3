NayePankh Foundation - Project Overview
Youth-led non-profit organization dedicated to social welfare and community development.
Established during the COVID-19 pandemic to support underprivileged communities.
Focuses on education, healthcare awareness, food distribution, and clothing support.
Conducts menstrual hygiene awareness campaigns and women's empowerment initiatives.
Organizes community welfare programs and social impact drives across India.
Provides assistance to needy families, children, and marginalized groups.
Supports animal welfare through food distribution and care initiatives.
Driven by passionate volunteers committed to creating positive social change.
Has impacted more than 200,000+ lives through various programs and campaigns.
Promotes youth participation in nation-building and community service.
Features a modern and responsive NGO website built with React.js and Tailwind CSS.
Includes sections such as Hero, About, Programs, Impact, Contact, Donation, and Footer.
Uses Framer Motion for smooth animations and interactive user experience.
Supports mobile-friendly and responsive design across all devices.
Showcases the foundation's mission, vision, achievements, and ongoing initiatives.
Encourages volunteering, donations, and community participation.
Mission: "Giving Wings to Hope."
Vision: Building a better future through compassion, awareness, education, and collective action.
Technology Stack: React.js, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion, React Icons, and Vite.
Designed to increase awareness, engagement, and support for NayePankh Foundation's social causes.
